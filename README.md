# Jassi-AI-Business-Manager
Autonomous AI business manager for vehicle transportation. Handles trips, commissions, payments, driver reliability scoring and weekly reporting via Telegram. Built solo in 8 days using n8n + Claude AI.
What is Jassi?
Jassi is a fully autonomous AI business manager built for the vehicle transportation industry. It handles the entire operational lifecycle of a transport business — from trip logging and commission tracking to driver reliability scoring and automated weekly reporting — all through a simple Telegram interface.
No dashboards. No manual entry. Just message Jassi and it handles the rest.
Why I Built This
Managing a vehicle transportation business means tracking dozens of trips, multiple drivers, pending payments, and commission splits — all manually. Jassi eliminates that entirely. One message to the bot handles what used to take hours of spreadsheet work.
Core Features
Trip Management
Log new trips with driver, route, and fare details
Retrieve full trip history by driver or date range
Cancel trips and automatically adjust records
Financial Tracking
Calculate commissions per driver per trip
Track pending payments and outstanding balances
Generate daily and weekly financial summaries
Driver Intelligence
Reliability scoring based on trip completion and cancellation history
Driver performance reports on demand
Automated alerts for low-reliability drivers
Automated Reporting
Daily 9 AM briefing sent via Telegram
Weekly Monday summary of all business activity
Error monitoring with instant alert notifications
Memory System
Jassi remembers context across conversations
Fetches relevant history before every response
Stores all interactions for audit trail
Stack:
Automation: n8n (self-hosted on Railway)
AI Brain: Claude API (Anthropic)
Interface: Telegram Bot
Storage: Airtable / Google Sheets
Hosting: Railway
What Makes Jassi Different
Most business tools require you to adapt to them. Jassi adapts to you. It understands natural language, remembers your business context, and takes action — not just advice.
This is not a chatbot. This is an operating system for a small business.
